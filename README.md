## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Business Analyst case study focused on analyzing retail customer behavior, identifying revenue patterns, and improving customer retention strategies.

The project simulates a real-world business environment where raw transactional data is transformed into actionable insights using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The retail company faced the following challenges:

- Declining customer retention  
- Heavy reliance on discount-based acquisition  
- Uneven revenue contribution across customers  
- Lack of visibility into customer purchasing behavior  

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to:

- Identify high-value customer segments  
- Understand customer churn behavior  
- Analyze impact of discounts on revenue and retention  
- Improve marketing and retention strategies using data  
- Provide actionable business recommendations  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- Python (Pandas, NumPy) → Data cleaning, preprocessing, EDA  
- SQL (PostgreSQL) → Business queries, segmentation, analysis  
- Power BI → Dashboard creation and visualization  
- Excel → Data validation and quick analysis  

---

## 🔍 Approach

### 1. Data Cleaning (Python)
- Handled missing values and duplicates  
- Standardized customer and product data  
- Created key business metrics such as:
  - Total spend per customer  
  - Purchase frequency  
  - Average order value  
  - Recency  

---

### 2. Business Analysis (SQL)
- Customer segmentation analysis  
- Revenue contribution analysis  
- Discount vs non-discount comparison  
- Product category performance analysis  

---

### 3. Analytical Methods
- RFM Segmentation (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)  
- Cohort Analysis for retention tracking  
- Churn analysis based on behavior and category  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Top 20% of customers contribute ~70% of total revenue  
- Discount-driven customers show ~35–40% lower retention  
- Certain product categories show significantly higher churn rates  
- Early repeat purchase behavior strongly increases customer lifetime value  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Reduce dependency on blanket discount strategies  
- Focus on retaining high-value customers  
- Introduce second-purchase incentive programs  
- Improve performance in high-churn product categories  
- Use customer segmentation (RFM) for targeted marketing  

---

## 📈 Business Impact (Expected)

If implemented, the recommendations can lead to:

- 25–30% reduction in unnecessary discount spending  
- 20–25% increase in customer lifetime value  
- 15–20% improvement in retention rates  
- Improved marketing ROI through segmentation
